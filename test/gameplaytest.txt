## double down
when player double downs, draws only one card. cannot hit after. 

## splits 
when player is dealt the same card they can split, treating it as two hands. (10s, jacks, queens, kings are all treated the same as a 10) 
when player splits an ace, they only draw one card for each. No additional hit. (essentially a double after split) 

## Surrender 
player gives up and loses half of their bet (when credits are implemented) 

## buttons
ensure buttons work properly. when hit is pressed, player draws an additional card. when stand is pressed, player doesn't draw cards and dealer reveals their hiddne card. when double, player only draws one card and stands. when split, player splits their cards into two different hands and they get to play like normal.

new game button - starts a new game and reshuffles deck.
