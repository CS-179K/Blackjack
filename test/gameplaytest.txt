## double down
when player double downs, draws only one card. cannot hit after. 

## splits 
when player is dealt the same card they can split, treating it as two hands. (10s, jacks, queens, kings are all treated the same as a 10) 
when player splits an ace, they only draw one card for each. No additional hit. (essentially a double after split) 

## Surrender 
player gives up and loses half of their bet (when credits are implemented)
